Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my DSA progress?

You can track your DSA progress through our new DSA Tracker feature. Navigate to the DSA Tracker page from the sidebar menu, where you can mark problems as "Not Started," "In Progress," or "Solved." The tracker also provides statistics on your performance across different platforms like LeetCode, GeeksForGeeks, CodeChef, and Codeforces.

How do I request leave?

To request leave, navigate to the Leave Form page from the sidebar or pinned menu. Click on the "Request Leave" button, select the leave type (Casual Leave or Sick Leave), specify the dates, provide a reason, and submit your request. You can track the status of your leave requests in the Leave History section.

How can I collaborate with other students?

You can collaborate with other students through our Community feature. Navigate to the Community page, where you can join existing groups or create new ones. Within these groups, you can share resources, work on projects together, and communicate with your peers in a WhatsApp-like interface.

How do I create my portfolio?

To create your portfolio, go to the Portfolio page from the sidebar or pinned menu. You'll find tools to showcase your projects, skills, and achievements. You can customize your portfolio card, which will be visible to other students and potential employers. Don't forget to add a link to your personal portfolio website if you have one.

How can I change the theme of the platform?

You can change the theme of the platform by going to the Settings page, accessible from the profile dropdown in the top-right corner. In the Settings page, you'll find a theme toggle option that allows you to switch between dark and light themes according to your preference.
