Event Details

Annual Hackathon 2023: Code for Change

June 15, 2023
9:00 AM - 9:00 PM (24 hours)
Main Campus, Innovation Hub
Tech

Join us for our annual 24-hour hackathon where you'll collaborate with fellow students to build innovative solutions addressing real-world problems. This year's theme is "Technology for Sustainable Development".

Whether you're a coding expert or just starting out, this event welcomes participants of all skill levels. Form teams of 2-4 members and put your creativity and problem-solving skills to the test!

Event Highlights

24-hour coding challenge with mentorship from industry professionals
Prizes worth ₹50,000 to be won across multiple categories
Free meals, snacks, and refreshments throughout the event
Networking opportunities with industry recruiters
Certificates for all participants

Requirements

Participants must bring their own laptops and necessary equipment
Valid college ID is required for entry
Teams must register before June 10, 2023
